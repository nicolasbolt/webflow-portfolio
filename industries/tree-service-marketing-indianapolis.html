Frequently Asked Questions

1

What if I don't have time to send you photos and videos every week?

We get it, you are very busy. Because of this, we make it very easy on you. All you have to do is take a few photos and a short video while you are on the job site and send them via text/email. Everything else is handled for you.

2

What if my work isn't interesting enough to take before/after pictures of?

For customers looking for a contractor they can trust, having social media posts or website pages dedicated to explaining how you keep the customer's property safe, how you ensure the quality of work is up to standard, etc is very interesting to them. It might be basic for you but for local homeowners these things aren't obvious.

3

Will this sound like me or like AI or a marketing company wrote it?

We take your photos, videos of you explaining your work in order to collect the experience and expertise that you have and build a knowledge base. We will use your exact words and pull from your experiences to write content that is completely unique to you and talks about your actual work experience. We don't post generic marketing content only real experiences and real proof of your work.
