How long does it take to see results?

It depends on how established your business is. For businesses with an established offline marketing strategy and good branding, we can see good results within a couple months. For newer businesses it can take a 6+ month timeline. SEO is an asset that compounds with time. Once you have the system in place it will continue to pay dividends for years to come.

Will AI make SEO useless and outdated?

That is not what we are seeing. Website traffic for blog posts has been going down but when customers need a tree removed, they are still going to company websites.
