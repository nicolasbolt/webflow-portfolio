Get Clarity on How to Find Customers Online

The 15-minute workbook that shows Indianapolis contractors exactly where they're losing customers online (and how to fix it)

Throwing Money at Marketing With Nothing to Show For It?

You're spending money on a website. Maybe running some ads. Asking customers for reviews when you remember. But you have no clue if any of it is actually bringing in new jobs.

Meanwhile, the big national franchises have entire marketing departments tracking every click and call. You're figuring this out alone, between estimates and job sites.

The truth? Most local contractors are losing customers because of simple, fixable gaps they don't even know exist. A slow website. An incomplete Google profile. Inconsistent business info across directories. These aren't huge problems, but they're costing you jobs every single week.

Finally Get Clear on What's Costing You Customers

I've audited dozens of Indianapolis home service businesses over the past few months. The same expensive mistakes kept showing up over and over.

So I built this workbook to walk you through exactly what to look for in your own marketing, no tech skills or marketing degree required.

  • Website Health Check - Find the gaps costing you customers (slow load times, missing contact info, poor mobile experience)
  • Google Business Profile Audit - Make sure you're showing up when people search "[your service] near me"
  • Review Generation System - A simple process to get more 5-star reviews without being pushy
  • Local Listing Checklist - Ensure your business info is consistent everywhere (inconsistencies confuse Google and customers)
