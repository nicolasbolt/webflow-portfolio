Do I own my own website?

Yes, fully. If you ever leave, you take the site with you. We don't hold it hostage or charge extra to transfer it.

What if I don't have a Google Business Profile yet?

No problem, setup and optimization of your GBP is included in every plan. We handle the whole thing.

There is no exact answer for this but typically GBP improvements start coming in 30–60 days, and organic rankings start to see results after 90–120 days.
