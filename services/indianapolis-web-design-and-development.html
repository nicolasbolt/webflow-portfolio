How I Build Websites For Contractors

I Research Your Market Before Building Anything

Most contractors get a website that looks nice but doesn't bring in work. That's because the site isn't built on strategy. It's just guessing.

I start every project with deep competitor research and SEO analysis. I study what homeowners in your area actually search for when they need your services. I analyze the top-ranking contractors to find gaps you can exploit.

Then I map out a complete content strategy:

  • Service pages that target the exact searches your customers make
  • Location pages optimized for every city you serve
  • A site structure designed to rank and convert, not just look pretty

This isn't template work. Every site is custom-built around real search data for your specific market.

I Build Websites That Turn Visitors Into Paying Customers

Your website has one job: generate leads. Everything else is secondary. I design sites that guide visitors to contact you. Clear calls-to-action on every page. Contact forms that are impossible to miss. Mobile experience that works perfectly because that's where most searches happen.

The technical foundation matters too. Fast load times under 2 seconds. Clean code that Google can crawl easily. Schema markup so your reviews and services show up in search results. Every site I build scores 90+ on Google PageSpeed.

Home service businesses need leads, not awards for design. I build sites that do the actual work of growing your business.
