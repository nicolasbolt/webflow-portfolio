Indianapolis Pest Control

Premier Pest Control

When I met the owners of Premier Pest Control, they had just taken over the business from the previous owner and they had a Wordpress website that had room for improvement. I rebuilt their website from the ground up to be: SEO optimized, built to convert website visitors into paying customers, and have analytics in order to track success of the project.

See the Website

The Problem

With an outdated website, they had no idea if their website was bringing them leads. Without any service or location pages, they were limited in how many opportunities they had to show up in search results.

The Solution

I completed a full redesign of their website. Pages were created for every location that they do work in and every service they provide. I setup analytics and tracking to provide them with data to show the exact results their website is creatng for their business.

The Result

They now are getting calls and form submissions from people who are finding them on Google. They have a blog to continue updating their site with new additional helpful content. After handing the project off to them, I provided them with all the resources necessary to make content updates to their site on their own without having to rely on me.
