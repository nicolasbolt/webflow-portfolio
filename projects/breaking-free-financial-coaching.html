Breaking Free Financial Coaching

Breaking Free Financial Coaching is a new personal financial coaching business based out of Hendricks County. Josh, the owner, wanted a website to build credibility, showcase results, and guide prospects to a free discovery call. I custom designed his website to achieve these goals.

See the Website

The Problem

Without a website, Josh was not building the credibility online that he wanted. He needed a place to store reviews, showcase his results (total wealth created and total debt paid off).

The Solution

I custom designed a website for Josh that solved these problems. I also built him a custom counter animation that would count up to his totals to create more engagement with the website. I built a backend system that will allow him to add reviews and make updates on his own as his business grows.

The Result

After launch, I provided him with support and guidance on next steps. We can now track results and see his online presence grow.
