Indianapolis Roof Replacement & Roof Repair

Finish Line Roofing and Exteriors

When Finish Line Roofing & Exteriors reached out, they had no website, no Google presence, and no way for homeowners to find them. In a market dominated by established competitors with hundreds of reviews, they needed to establish credibility and build a solid online presence.

See the Website See the Design File

The Problem

As a new roofing company based out of Indianapolis, they needed a way to be more visible online. Despite quality work and proper certifications, they weren't very visible outside of their Facebook page.

The Solution

I approached this project in 3 phases. First I researched the market and determined what Indianapolis customers were searching for. Then I put together a design file so they could see what the website would look like and provide feedback.  Finally I built the entire site based on their feedback and handled getting it live on their domain name.

The Result

After completion of this project, they have more credibility, a foundation to build their SEO on, and pages for every location/service they offer so customers can easily understand what they do and where they work.
